ABOUT ME

My name is Gaurang Jhanwar and I am an 18 years old Indian studying Business Administration at ESSEC Asia Pacific in Singapore . I was born and grew up in Kolkata and have been in Singapore for one semester! I stay alone in Singapore. I absolutely love animals, horses in particular. I studied in LMB, an Interntional school in Kolkata before pursuing a GBBA in ESSEC Business School. I chose this university amongst others because I loved the unique multicultural experience ESSEC offers. I'm keen to work in the Economics and Finance industry and I have been working towards this goal for the past few years.

I really enjoy travelling to new places and learning about different cultures and trying new cuisines! My days are incomplete without music and meeting my friends and most importantly - Netflix. I love watching new shows and listening to music. Additionally, if I'm able to find the time, I'm an adventorous person and i love to cycle and swim .
